Home page

Hello! Welcome.

At the neighbor's restaurant, you can feel at home. We have many options in our meal menu to meet our audience. What would you like to eat today? Make your request right now!The neighbor's restaurant is a restaurant that provides lunch at a price that everyone can afford. Through the site, there are our contacts and photos of our dishes. Take advantage and choose your lunch without leaving home! We want our neighborhood to enjoy the tastiest food in the region with Brazilian cuisine such as feijoada, arroz tropeiro, moqueca bahiana, etc. All this so that our friends and customers can enjoy our flavors and have contact with the Brazilian root through taste. Since 2013, we have been in the market proving the value of Brazilian food and showing America that Brazilian food can be part of the daily life of American families. We came here to stay and expand our business. We already have two units and we are expanding and growing. Always with this proposal of bringing quality and comfort to our customers, our facilities have a space for people of all tribes and we value diversity because we believe it is necessary for the growth and good of our society as a whole. We also have live music so that our clients can have a moment of leisure as well. Through music, we provide an environment of post-work relaxation and a pleasant and enjoyable weekend.

About

Would you like to know more about our dishes?

We have all the information about how the dishes are made. Prices for each meal are also available here. We have a safe environment for your children, we have a playground! The kids will love it. We are a serious company with more than 15 years of tradition and we are in the market to make a difference and help Brazilian cuisine to enter the homes of all the residents of America. Our playground is the main part where children interact with each other, avoiding the use of devices such as tablets, smartphones, and laptops, helping in the socialization and development of your child. If you have a busy and busy life, you can make reservations and orders for your week, which will help you take care of your health, because fast food will be forgotten after you taste our meals, together with the practice of physical activity, will guarantee physical and mental benefits for you. We have Wi-Fi in our rooms, comfortable chairs and tables, and a special place for handicapped people to enjoy a good meal too.
